How to Use Emil Isavia
1. Generate Bookings

Pull real flight data from external APIs to create Premium valet customer bookings. Each booking represents a customer who will drop off their car before departure and expect it ready when they return.

2. Run Optimization

Use GurobiPy to solve the staff scheduling and car movement problem. The optimizer decides which storage zone each car goes to and when all moves should happen, minimizing total staff time while ensuring cars are ready on time.

3. Simulate Operations

Test the optimized plan using SimPy discrete-event simulation. This adds realistic delays, variability, and stochastic elements to validate the plan works under real conditions.

Key Constraints
  • Cars must be at Delivery ≥15 minutes before customer returns
  • Zone capacities: Reception(14), Gull(50), P3(150), Delivery(20)
  • Limited staff (max 10) with realistic travel/walk times
