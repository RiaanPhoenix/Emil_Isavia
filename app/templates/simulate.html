What This Does

The simulation runs multiple stochastic replications of a day's operations using a SimPy discrete-event model. Each run introduces random flight delays and drive-time variability to test how well an optimization plan holds up.

Optimizer vs Baseline: When comparison is enabled, the simulation also runs a simple FIFO heuristic (park in nearest available zone, no forward scheduling) and compares service levels and violations.

Key metric: Service level = % of cars delivered on time (≥15 min lead time).
